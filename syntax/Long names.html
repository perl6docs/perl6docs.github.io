(Signature) Long names

To exclude certain parameters from being considered in multiple dispatch, separate them with a double semicolon.

multi sub f(Int $i, Str $s;; :$b) { say "$i, $s, {$b.perl}" };
f(10, 'answer');
# OUTPUT: «10, answer, Any␤»
